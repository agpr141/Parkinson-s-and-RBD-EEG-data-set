Parkinson’s and RBD EEG data set

Data Description

This dataset contains 57 channel scalp EEG data from four participant groups; Parkinsons Disease (n=11), Parkinson’s Disease with REM Sleep Behaviour Disorder (n=6), REM Sleep Behaviour Disorder (n=16) and Healthy Controls (n=19). EEG recordings encompassed different arousal states: quiet wakefulness and 4 stages of sleep (N1, N2 , N3 and REM, scored according to AASM guidelines). Anonymised participant details and timings of arousal states are available alongside the EEG.

Accessing the data

We are in the process of making the dataset available on the data.Bris Research Data Repository (https://data.bris.ac.uk/data/about). This site will be updated with instructions to access the data once it is available on the data.Bris Research Data Repository. In order to access the data you will need to state your name, the purpose of your research, how you will be using the data and how you will ensure that the data is appropriately protected.
